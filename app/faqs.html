FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Answer all of your questions

Admission

  • What is the education Language in Ukraine Universities

    Education language in Ukraine Universities is Russian and English.

  • Do i have to get Preparation Course?

    First you have to make a desicion of what language you want to study in. If you choosen language skill is poor you have to get 10 month preparation course to learn this language, and then start your degree study.

  • What I need to start application process?

    You have to fill the application form on our website and send to us below documents scans.
    If you apply for Bachelor Degree:
    Passport
    Secondary School Certificate
    If you apply for Master Degree:
    Passport
    Secondary School Certificate
    Bachelor Diploma
    If your documents are originally in English language, then you can send the scans as they are, otherwise if original language of your documents is different, you have to send translated into Russian or Ukrainian languages copies.

  • How long does it take to process documents?

    It will take 7 business days to process your documents and send the hard copy of your invitation letter.

  • What additional charges I will face with?

    The additional charge you will have is courier services to get the hard copy of an invitation letter and visa fee. We are working with the students from all over the world and the cost of these services differs from country to country, that's why we can't handle it as well.

Living in Ukraine

  • What is the fee of On Campus Accommodation?

    On campus accomodation starts from 250$ per year, it depends on University and options you choose.

  • What is the average fee of Private Accommodation?

    Students can rent one bedroom private accomodation for for 150$ - 300$ per Month.

  • What is the average monthly expences?

    Average monthly expences is about 200-300$ except accomodation rental fees.

  • How is it, to be another nationality in Ukraine?

    There's absolutely no racism and nationalism, Kharkiv is the city of students and more that 66000 international students from all over the world are studying here nowadays.
