NATIONAL AEROSPACE UNIVERSITY ``KHARKIV AVIATION INSTITUTE``

Since, there are many universities nowadays that offer excellent and quality education for the students, Ukraine truly become the education hub in various fields of study. Have you wonder of flying the helicopter or airplane, the feelings of their amazing? Doing that kind of course and learning all their functionalities can be an awesome and overwhelming feeling. Kharkiv Aviation Institute is the university in Ukraine that provides great knowledge in every subject of Helicopter and Airplane Designing courses.

The Aviation professionals and experts work in many different departments in aviation industry. The different departments are system engineering, aircraft design, search & rescue, test piloting, air-traffic control, human factors, Computer system and electrical engineering.

GET DEGREE IN AVIONICS ENGINEERING

One of degree programs in Kharkiv Aviation Institute (National Aerospace University) is the Avionic Engineering, where students are being exposed to technical skill and engineering sets which are essential in the industry, like; aircraft power & distribution system, avionic system design, aircraft pulse & radar system, traffic alert & avoidance system, flight instrument system and computer aided designs.

The avionic is one of the branch or division of engineering that deals with electronic device which is being used in spacecraft, artificial satellites and aircrafts. The subject that is being taught in engineering are technical thermodynamics, chemical & physical bases of technology process, aero hydrodynamics, thermo physical property of substance, fluid & gas mechanics etc.

The helicopters and aircraft designing is the process that deals in designing engineering field, such as electrical, electronic and mechanical engineering, that comes under aircraft engineering and avionics field.
